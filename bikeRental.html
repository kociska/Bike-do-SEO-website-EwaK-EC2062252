Welcome to the Bike King Borders bike rental. Here you'll find our range of bikes and accessories to hire out for a day trip or weekly holidays in Borders. We've got over 70 bikes to choose from. Full offer - on site, so we encourage you to visit us, talk on site and take a test ride.

We rent road, triathlon, mountain and cross bikes as well as various types of sports equipment: time trial helmets, carbon wheels, suitcases for transporting the bike on the plane. Equipment can be rented at our stationary store in Kelso.
All the bikes we offer are fully functional and equipped with appropriate clipless pedals, bottle cages and an emergency repair kit (inner tube, pump). We try to fulfill the wishes of every cyclist. About Us

Bike King Borders is a growing company based in the Borders region of Scotland.

Our Mission

The company sell and repair road, mountain and hybrid bikes as well as sell a range of bike accessories and clothing. In addition, they hire out bikes for daily use on nearby off-road bike trails.

Our Vision

The company are unique in the Borders due to their location and having the benefit of brilliant cycle trails on their doorstep. They pride themselves in selling only quality products and guaranteeing servicing and repair.
